Our Services

At Hazi Abdul Awal High School, we offer a range of services aimed at providing students with a well-rounded educational experience. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to delivering high-quality teaching and mentorship, ensuring that each student receives personalized attention and support.

From comprehensive academic programs to extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and community service opportunities, we strive to nurture the holistic development of our students.

Additionally, we provide counseling and guidance services to support students in their personal and academic growth journey. Our aim is to empower students with the skills, knowledge, and values they need to succeed in their future endeavors.
